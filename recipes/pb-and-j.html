PB & J Recipe

Description

The PB&J is as American as it gets, and its story is spread across the internet from web page to web page as if by digital butter knife, each as recognizable to the next as the sandwich itself.

This is the best PB&J recipe you will find on the internet!

Ingredients

  • (2) slices of bread
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 jar of jelly

Steps

  1. Spread a sufficient amount of peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread a sufficient amount of jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together.
  4. Enjoy!
